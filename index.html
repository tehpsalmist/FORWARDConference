Saturday, May 13 | 9:30am-12:30pm
700 N. Main St., Sellersville, PA

Faith Christian Academy's FORWARD Conference 2017 is an event aimed at helping high school students and their parents move forward confidently into college, career, and beyond.

Workshop Speakers

Each speaker will bring a focused perspective on preparing students for their next step beyond high school. The workshops will cover a broad range of topics: financial, spiritual, occupational, academic, and more. We are confident that both parents and students will find each session informational and inspirational.

The Exhibits

Throughout the entire event, the exhibits will be open in the gymnasium for parents and students to pick up information from a session they may have missed, learn about a variety different resources, and engage 1-on-1 with the experts and professionals of college and career planning. In addition to our workshop speakers, we have also invited several other professionals to offer their knowledge and expertise at the exhibits.

Ben DeClerico

Ben is the Math Department Head at Faith Christian Academy, and he has dedicated significant time to helping students successfully approach the SAT Math test. He is offering summer courses to students throughout the local community seeking to raise their scores.
